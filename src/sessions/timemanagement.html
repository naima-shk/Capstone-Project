TIME MANAGEMENT

DESCRIPTION


Time Management : In order to increase productivity through efficiency and effectiveness, deliberate efforts are made to exercise control of time spent on the activities because it helps bring more discipline and balance in life. While practicing time management focus is the main key. There are 7 ways for better time management:

  • Focus should be clear.
  • Prepare a task list that you need to complete.
  • Put first things first.
  • Procrastination should be dealt strictly.
  • Focus on one task at a time (stop multitasking).
  • eview and compare your completed tasks with the check list.
